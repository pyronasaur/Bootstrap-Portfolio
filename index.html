About Me

Hi! My name is Nick Pyron, and I am a web developer. I have 5+ years in the private sector developing solutions for clients while operating as a consultant. A fair number of those projects have been web applications, but many were written as plug-ins or standalone applications written in java or C#.NET. I enjoy mobile and web development and I am here to grow my skill set as a web guy.

I have many hobbies as I like to learn new things. When I am not coding I like to play guitar, train my dogs (we have four), paint miniature models, and go snowboarding if I get the chance. There isn't much chance in GA, but I like it enough to mention it. There are plenty of other things I like to do as well, but my wife and I have an 18 month old baby in the house and she really gets around! That takes me away from my other interests, but it is worth it.
